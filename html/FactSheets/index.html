Application Business Capability Map
Application Business Capability Maps are created from the Veterans Health Administration Information Systems Architecture (VistA) Capability Mapping Project (V-CAMP) within the Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) to assist with the VistA modernization efforts and to facilitate portfolio rationalization. Application maps will be developed for 33 of the VistA applications that have been identified by the V-CAMP Performance Work Statement (PWS). The Application Business Capability Maps are a snapshot of the analysis being conducted and do not represent the full analysis. As new information is identified by the V-CAMP team, new maps will be available for distribution. For additional details please contact Ms. Jennifer Freese, Director, V-CAMP, Jennifer.Freese@va.gov.

Data Objects

This section reflects the data objects that this application creates, modifies, or reads as part of normal operations. These data elements roll up into data objects, such as Patient ID. Data objects that are unique and only provided by this application are identified by boldfaced text.
